
# Intro

I studied computer science at the Federal University of Lavras (UFLA), and after graduating I worked with web development using open source software. After some years of experience, I decided to start a Master's degree in the field of computational intelligence. In the Master's I worked with simulations using numerical methods, more specifically with computational mesh generation. I worked with C/C++ language.

Finishing my Master's degree, I started teaching at the Federal University of Mato Grosso (UFMT), where I have been teaching computer science subjects since 2015. In 2018 I started my Ph.D at the Federal University of Goiás (UFG), where I have work with speech processing, e.g. Text-to-Speech (TTS), Speech-to-Text (STT), Speech Denoiser and Speech Recognition (SR).

# Projects

Working with CyberLabs support, we developed neural models for TTS and STT and SR in Brazilian Portuguese. The main models we work are Tacotron-2, Tacotron-GST, for TTS, and DeepSpeech2, Wave2Letter, Jasper, and now Wav2vec for STT.

# Currently

I am currently still developing my Ph.D. at UFG. In our partnership with Cyberlabs we are improving our applications for speech processing. I also continue teaching at UFMT.
